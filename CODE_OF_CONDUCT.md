# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the KubeNAS community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- being respectful and constructive
- accepting feedback gracefully
- focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, and discrimination.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Reporting

Report incidents to maintainers through private channels listed in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
